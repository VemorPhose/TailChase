# Code of Conduct

TailChase is a developer tool for careful debugging work. The project works best when discussion stays specific, respectful, and evidence-based.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of people with different experience levels and workflows.
- Critique code, docs, and decisions without personal attacks.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce a problem.
- Remove secrets, private logs, and customer details before posting.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Posting private credentials, logs, or personal information.
- Repeated off-topic promotion or disruptive comments.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who make the project unsafe for others.
